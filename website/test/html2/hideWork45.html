Warren Charles

An interactive experience of Head in the Clouds, the album from Los Angeles based rock quartet Warren Charles. Through this app you can stream the entire album using the custom analog media player, find hidden objects in the first of its kind memory photo hunt game, search the album artwork to access exciting app features, watch exclusive content within the hand painted video player, browse behind the scenes photos from recording sessions, read fun facts about the band - among other features.

Technologies

Stream audio from server, Tapjoy, StoreKit.framework, cocos2d, Flurry, Facebook iOS SDK, CoreImage Framework.
