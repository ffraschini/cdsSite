Push it Poker

One of our masterpieces! A complex application developed completely by us from ground up. It is a multiplayer iPhone app that lets people play poker online. You can have a list of contacts to play with, chat while playing the games, and invite friends via Facebook.

Technologies

IOS SDK, PHP, MySQL, Facebook API, Facebook iOS SDK, AddressBook Framework, AdWhirl, InMobi, ZestADZ, Greystripe Ads, Mobclix, Push Notifications, JSONKit, Multiple versions (free and paid) using same code.
