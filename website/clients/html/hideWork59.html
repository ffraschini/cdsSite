Vera+ app (Antel)

We developed the front-end of Vera+ Android mobile app. The app will allow visualization of live channels transmitted by Antel, diverse videos and programming will be displayed by these channels. The app has two channels: vera + (with technology and science content) and sports vera + (basically contains everything related to the world cup).

Technologies

Java + Android SDK, VideoView (for HLS and Progressive Download streaming), AndroidAnnotations, Volley, Gson, Intellij IDEA 13, Genymotion
