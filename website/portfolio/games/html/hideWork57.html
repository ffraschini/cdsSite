Weev

We are working on the development of this iOS app. Weev is social video that talks back. Ask any question, and have the world answer it in short videos that are stitched together to give you all the insight you need. And, if you are too shy to ask, knock yourself out responding to as many questions you like.

Technologies

Front-end: Objective-C; UIKit for UI;The whole video/audio playback and recording is built with AVFoundation; Amazon Web Service SDK for iOS to communicate with our Amazon bucket; NSURLConnection to communicate with our server Backend: NodeJS, MongoDB, Amazon EC2, CloudFront, S3
