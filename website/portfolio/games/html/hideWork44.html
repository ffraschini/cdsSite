eJudged

An app that provides car show event managers with a time-saving practice that increases efficiency while decreasing overhead. This app (which calculates results in real-time) streamlines and smooths out the judging process, enhancing the success of show promoters, managers and entrants.

Technologies

Ruby on Rails, Objective-C, Xcode AFnetworking, SQLite, Security Framework, MySQL, Workbench, Openshift, Libraries (Paperclip, CSV, Git).
