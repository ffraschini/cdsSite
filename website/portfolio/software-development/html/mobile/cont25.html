• Next Generation Education is a tutoring program for children between 6 and 17 years old divided into two groups: primary learning (from 6 to 12 years old) and secondary learning (from 13 to 17 years old). The system includes two big learning topics: Mathematics and English (grammar, reading and writing). For each topic there are different levels that the student will have to pass and depend on the age of the student. After a level is completed, the score is calculated depending on which a different animation will be displayed. Therefore, the system also required the design of animations with a carnival theme. The scores obtained by the student may be redeemed for different predefined prizes.
