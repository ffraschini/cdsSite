• Fodessa is a group ecommerce website for gifts where you can add all your contacts from different social networks and register important occasions. This platform will not only remind you of these important occasions, but will suggest gift ideas, based on your contacts’ wish lists, and provide you with shipping information and a budget. Basically, it will significantly reduce the time required to do all your gift shopping! “Fodessa aims to pioneer the first platform that can perform your shopping for special occasions and holidays”

    We’ve been working on re-designing and developing this ecommerce website. We’ve also developed its iOS mobile application from scratch.
