edMobility

CRM (Customer Relationship Management) for schools. The client noticed the need to have a system to help organize and have better control over school charity functions, campaigns, and fundraisers. Through this CRM, all people who are related to the school (parents, students, teachers, etc) can connect with others and add/share relevant information with their group.

Technologies

Java, Jersey, REST webservices, HTML, Javascript, JQuery, CSS, MySQL (Plataforms: Java Virtual Machine, PhoneGap, Sencha)
