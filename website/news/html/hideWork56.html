Toymail

We are working on the development of this iOS app and developing the Android version from scratch. Now you can send mail to kids' toys! You simply record a voice message using the Toymail app from your smartphone, hit send, and our Mailmenª toys will speak them back in a funny voice (or your voice if you prefer). Kids can reply to you right from their toys.

Technologies

Front-end: Objective-C, Dirac - audio processing, Crashlytics - Crash report system, AFNetworking - networking framework, BlinkUp framework - Is a private framework that allows you to communicate with the toymail toys. Back-end: NodeJS, MongoDB, Memcached, Mocha, Jade
