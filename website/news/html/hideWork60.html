RadU

We developed this University Radiology application. It has videos 15-minute video courses on various topics related to Radiology. Users can view the videos, download slides, take exams and see their results, as well check calendar and events related to radiology.

Technologies

Facebook SDK, MediaPlayer, Quicklook, NSURLConnection (native), JSON, Parsing, Testflight, UITableView, UIViews, MFSlideMenu
